My Favorite Classical Music Pieces

Classical Music has always been the love of my life. When I was young, I would fall asleep listening to it on my radio. My favorite way to relax is by playing a masterpiece on the piano.

Here are some of the classical works that I love the most—composed by the top 5 musical minds I most deeply admire.

Franz Liszt:Liebesträum No.3

Frédéric Chopin:Nocturne Op. 9 No. 2

Dmitri Shostakovich:Waltz No. 2

George Frideric Handel:The Messiah

Giuseppe Verdi:Messa da Requiem

Johann Sebastian Bach:Prelude and Fugue in C minor
